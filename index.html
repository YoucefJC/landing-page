Meet the Mediterranean meal plan

The world’s most well-studied eating pattern, the Mediterranean diet comes with many health benefits — from improving heart and brain health, to lowering the risk of diabetes and certain cancers.

Why is the Mediterranean diet so good for you?

Lowering your risk of cardiovascular disease, including a heart attack or stroke.

Supporting a body weight that’s healthy for you.

Supporting healthy blood sugar levels, blood pressure and cholesterol.

Supporting a healthy balance of gut microbiota in your digestive system.

The Mediterranean diet is a healthy-eating plan. It's focused on plants and includes the traditional flavors and cooking methods of the region.
